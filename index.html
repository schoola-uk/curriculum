About Schoola

Schoola (schoola.uk) is a free, independent curriculum signposting tool for UK primary school pupils aged 5–11 and their parents. We map the full statutory National Curriculum (KS1 and KS2) and curate links to the best free educational resources.

Schoola does not create or host its own content. We link to BBC Teach, Oak National Academy, BBC Bitesize, Twinkl, White Rose Maths, and other trusted UK education providers.

The AI Tutor feature is powered by Anthropic's Claude and is designed to help pupils and parents understand curriculum topics — it does not replace professional teaching advice.
